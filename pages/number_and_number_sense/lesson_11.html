Lesson 11: Multiplying mentally 2 Digit by 1 Digit numbers with Products up to 200 Explore and Discover!

How will you mentally multiply 2-digit by 1-digit numbers ?

Study the examples : Multiply 3 by 48 mentally .

Heres how to do it :

Solution 1

Mentally multiply the ones                    3 x 8 = 24

Mentally multiply tens by ones             3 x 40 = 120

Mentally add the partial products      24 + 120 = 144

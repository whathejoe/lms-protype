Lesson 21 - Dividing Mentally 2- to 3-Digit Numbers by 1-Digit Numbers without remainder Explore and Discover!

Camille placed 630 mangoes equally in 7 baskets. How many mangoes should each basket have?

Can you solve the problem mentally?

Think of basic division facts to do mental division

Do you know how to estimate?

Study the solutions below:

• Think of basic division facts if the dividend and the divisor are compatible numbers.

• Then, annex the zero in the dividend to the quotient.

Thus, 630 ÷ 7 = 90

Answer: There will be 90 mangoes in each basket.

Now, let us try another one.

Divide 231 by 7.

Study the solution.

• Since we cannot see easily basic division facts, we can rename the dividend in such way that the renamed dividend is compatible with 7.

Thus, 231 ÷ 7 = 33

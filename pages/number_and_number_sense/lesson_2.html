Lesson 2: Place Value and Value of a Digit in Numbers up to 100 000 Explore and Discover!

In 2013, Barangay San Sebastian harvested 164 789 kilograms of mangoes.

What does each digit in 64 789 mean?

Study how 64 789 is written in the place value chart

The digit 9 is in the ones place. Its value is 9

The digit 8 is in the tens place. Its value is 80

The digit 7 is in the hundreds place. Its value is 700

The digit 4 is in the one thousand place. Its value is 4 000

The digit 6 is in the ten thousand place. Its value is 60 00

The digit 1 is in the hundred thousand place. Its value is 100 000

The value of a digit is determined by multiplying that digit by its place value.

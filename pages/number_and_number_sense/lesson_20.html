In an orchard, Mr. Larry harvested 1694 mangoes. He plans to place 24 mangoes in a basket. About how many baskets does he need?

Do you know how to estimate?

Study the solutions below:

Rounding Method

Compatible Number Method

Actual Answer

Answer: He needs 70 baskets with 24 pieces each and one basket with 14 mangoes. If the compatible method is used, he needs about 80 baskets. If the rounding method Is used, he needs about 100 baskets.

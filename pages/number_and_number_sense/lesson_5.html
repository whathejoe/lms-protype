Lesson 5: Comparing Numbers up to 100 000 using Rational Symbols Explore and Discover!

Mang Mario is a farmer. Last year, he harvested 13 656 kilograms of palay. This year, he harvested 13 465 kilograms of palay.

Which year did he harvested more?

How many kilograms of palay did Mang Mario harvest last year? This year? Which year did he harvest more? How will you compare the given numbers?

When comparing two digit numbers, you can follow these steps:

1. Align the digits with the same place value.

2. Starting from the left, compare the digit in each place value.

3. The first pair of digits that are of different values will determine which number is greater or less.

Therefore, 13 656 > 13 465.

So, Mang Mario harvested more palay last year than this year.
